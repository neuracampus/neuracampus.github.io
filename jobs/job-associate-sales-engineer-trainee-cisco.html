Associate Sales Engineer Trainee

Company: Cisco

Location: Bangalore

Qualification: B.E / B.Tech

Experience: Freshers

CTC: 4-10LPA

Last Date: 2/15/2026

Job Description

As an Associate Sales Engineer Trainee you will learn how to progress deals through the Introduction, Discovery, and Custom Demonstration stages with guidance from experienced team members. Learn to collaborate with Key Account Managers and participate in marketing events, gaining exposure to successful event execution and client engagement strategies. Participate in client-facing activities such as presentations, proposals, business case development, and product demonstrations, building communication and technical skills. Develop foundational knowledge in Sales Analytics and Sales Process excellence by shadowing senior sales engineers and engaging in hands-on training. Observe and understand how leaders inspire teams to drive growth, foster accountability, and prepare accurate sales forecasts for senior management. Learn to prioritize initiatives and focus areas by adopting disciplined use of sales systems, tools, processes, and methodologies under mentorship. Participate in cross-functional collaborations and learn how to contribute to segment strategies and high-impact growth initiatives alongside senior leadership.
