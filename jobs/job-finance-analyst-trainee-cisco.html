Finance Analyst Trainee

Company: Cisco

Location: Bangalore

Qualification: Bachelor’s degree in Accounts / Finance

Experience: Freshers

CTC: 7-8 LPA

Last Date: 2/15/2026

Job Description

As a Finance Analyst Trainee, you will gain practical experience in building impactful dashboards, understanding Cisco’s business operations, participating in the month-end close process, and assisting with the consolidation of statutory accounts. You will develop key skills in financial analysis, reporting, and contribute to the team’s overall success. This is a 12-month apprenticeship program including on-the-job and classroom-based training. You will also have the opportunity to learn new reporting tools like Power BI and FinBO.
