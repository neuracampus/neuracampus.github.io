Top Companies Hiring Freshers in 2025

A complete guide for engineering and non-engineering graduates looking for verified off-campus opportunities.

Every year, thousands of fresh graduates worry about one big question: “Which companies are actually hiring freshers?” With campus placements becoming limited and competitive, off-campus hiring has become the most reliable path for freshers to start their careers.

In this blog, NeuraCampus brings you a carefully curated list of top companies hiring freshers in 2025. These companies are known for structured hiring programs, fresher-friendly roles, and transparent recruitment processes.

Why Off-Campus Hiring Is Important for Freshers

Off-campus hiring allows companies to reach a larger talent pool beyond college campuses. For freshers, this means:

  • More opportunities irrespective of college tier
  • Multiple chances throughout the year
  • Exposure to real industry-level recruitment processes

The companies listed below regularly conduct off-campus drives, apprenticeship programs, and fresher hiring initiatives.

1. Infosys

Infosys is one of India’s largest IT services companies and a consistent recruiter of fresh graduates.

Common Fresher Roles:

  • System Engineer
  • Process Executive
  • Digital Specialist Engineer

Infosys offers extensive training through its Mysuru campus, making it ideal for candidates with limited industry exposure.

CTC Range: ₹3.6 LPA – ₹8 LPA

2. Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)

TCS runs one of the most structured fresher hiring programs in India, including TCS NQT and off-campus recruitment drives.

Common Fresher Roles:

  • Assistant System Engineer
  • Graduate Trainee

TCS focuses on long-term career growth and internal mobility, making it suitable for freshers looking for stability.

CTC Range: ₹3.3 LPA – ₹7 LPA

3. Reliance Jio

Reliance Jio actively hires freshers for engineering, network operations, and digital roles.

  • Apprentice Engineer
